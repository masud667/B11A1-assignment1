Date: 13 Sep 2024 Time: 10.25 PM

Bangladesh marked one month since a youth-led movement ended Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina’s 15-year rule. Initially sparked by protests against a quota system for government jobs, the unrest soon escalated into a full-scale revolution. More than 1,000 people were killed, and more than 400 students lost their eyesight due to police brutality.
